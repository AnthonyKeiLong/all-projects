Welcome to the Theory of Psychosocial Development

Erik Erikson believed that personality develops in a predetermined order through eight stages of psychosocial development, from infancy to adulthood. During each stage, a person experiences a psychosocial crisis which could have a positive or negative outcome for personality development.
