Rainbow Six Siege: Mastering Bank

This lesson teaches how to attack and defend Bank with a full-round structure instead of isolated tips. Use the controls to swap sides, trigger round-changing situations, and watch the map update with utility, routes, drone locations, holds, and fallback plans.

3 Floors Bank rewards vertical control, especially over Open Area, Archives, CEO, and the basement site.
2 Game Plans Attackers build info then squeeze. Defenders delay space, protect denial, and force awkward clears.
7 Scenarios Simulate picks, lost drones, broken setups, and man-disadvantage rounds to practice adaptation.
